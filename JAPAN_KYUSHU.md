# Kyushu Nuclear Data

Kyushu also has demand data - see [JAPAN.md](JAPAN.md)

## Nuclear Power Generation

**URLs:** 
- Sendai: `https://www.kyuden.co.jp/php/nuclear/sendai/rename.php?A=s_power.fdat&B=ncp_state.fdat`
- Genkai: `https://www.kyuden.co.jp/php/nuclear/genkai/rename.php?A=g_power.fdat&B=ncp_state.fdat`

**Notes:** 
- Real-time nuclear plant data
- Sendai and Genkai nuclear power plants
- Per-unit data in 10MW units
- Timestamps in Japanese format
